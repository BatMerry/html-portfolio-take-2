The Best Movies According to Daniel Michicoff

My Top 3 Movies of All-Time

Christopher Nolan's "The Dark Knight Trilogy"

Artistic, emotional, entertaining piece of cinema of one of my favorite childhood superheros. Very re-watchable

The Big Lebowski

A cult classic but also a perfect movie. Very re-watchable

Avengers Infinity War/ Endgame

It took all my movies I love with many different storytelling themes and devices and combined over a decade of movie characters into one satisfying culmination of a movie. Very re-watchable
